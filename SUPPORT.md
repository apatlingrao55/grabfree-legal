## 2. Getting Started

### 2.1 Creating an Account

To use the App, you must register an account by providing:

| Step | Action | Details |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Download the App | Available on the Apple App Store and Google Play |
| 2 | Register | Provide a valid email address, a username (3–30 characters), and a password (minimum 8 characters) |
| 3 | Verify your email | Enter the one-time verification code (OTP) sent to your email |
| 4 | Complete onboarding | Select your primary geographic area to access local community boards and item listings |

### 2.2 Creating a Listing

To give away an item:

1. Open the App and navigate to the item listing section
2. Provide a title (5–200 characters) and select a category
3. Optionally add a description, up to 3 photos, a location, and an availability date
4. Submit your listing — it will be visible to users in your selected area

### 2.3 Claiming an Item

To claim an item listed by another user:

1. Browse listings in your area or search by category
2. Select an item you are interested in
3. Send a message to the listing owner to coordinate collection
4. Once collected, confirm the transaction in the App

## 3. Frequently Asked Questions

### 3.1 Account

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| How do I create an account? | Download the App, register with your email, verify via OTP, and complete onboarding. See Section 2.1. |
| How do I change my username? | You can update your username in your profile settings within the App. |
| How do I change my email address? | Contact us at support-approjects@pm.me to request an email address change. |
| How do I reset my password? | Use the "Forgot Password" option on the login screen. A reset code will be sent to your registered email. |
| How do I delete my account? | Use the account deletion feature within the App. This will permanently remove your data as described in our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY.md), Section 4.2. |

### 3.2 Listings and Items

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| How do I create a listing? | Open the App, navigate to the listing section, fill in the item details, and submit. See Section 2.2. |
| How many photos can I upload? | Up to 3 photos per listing. Photos are compressed and converted to WebP format. |
| How long do listings stay active? | Listings expire after 14 days if not claimed. You may relist an expired item up to 3 times. |
| How do I edit or delete a listing? | Open your listing and use the edit or delete options. Deleting a listing also removes associated photos. |
| Can I sell items on the App? | No. The App is strictly for free giveaways. Selling or requesting payment is prohibited under our [Terms of Use](TERMS_OF_USE.md). |

### 3.3 Messaging and Notifications

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| How do I message another user? | Navigate to an item listing and use the message option to contact the listing owner. |
| Are my messages private? | Yes. Messages are visible only to you and the other participant in the conversation. |
| How do I manage notifications? | Go to your notification preferences within the App. You can enable or disable notifications by type and set quiet hours. |

## 4. Troubleshooting

### 4.1 Common Issues

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Cannot log in | Verify your email and password are correct. Use "Forgot Password" if needed. Ensure your account has not been suspended. |
| Email verification code not received | Check your spam/junk folder. Request a new code. Ensure you entered the correct email address during registration. |
| App crashes or freezes | Ensure you are running the latest version of the App. Restart the App or your device. If the issue persists, contact us. |
| Photos not uploading | Check your internet connection. Ensure the App has permission to access your photo library. Try a smaller image. |
| Listing not appearing | Ensure your listing was submitted successfully. Check that you are viewing the correct geographic area. |

### 4.2 Account Issues

If your account has been suspended or banned, you will receive a notification with the reason. If you believe the suspension was made in error, contact us at support-approjects@pm.me with your username and the details of your case.

### 4.3 Email Quarantine

If your email is quarantined during registration, it is undergoing peer review by established community members. You will be informed of your quarantine status. If you need a manual review, contact us at support-approjects@pm.me.

## 5. Safety Tips

When using the App and meeting other users to exchange items, we recommend:

- **Meet in public**: Choose a well-lit, populated public location
- **Meet during the day**: Arrange exchanges during daylight hours
- **Tell someone**: Inform a friend or family member of your plans
- **Trust your instincts**: If something feels wrong, do not proceed with the exchange
- **Check the item**: Inspect the item before accepting it
- **Do not share personal information**: Avoid sharing your home address, financial details, or government IDs

For more information on user safety and your responsibilities, see our [Terms of Use](TERMS_OF_USE.md), Sections 12 and 15.
