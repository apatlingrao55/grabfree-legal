**Last Updated: March 3, 2026**

**BY CREATING AN ACCOUNT OR USING THE APP, YOU AGREE TO COMPLY WITH THIS ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY. IF YOU DO NOT AGREE, DO NOT USE THE APP.**

## 1. Introduction

GrabFree is a community-based platform for the free exchange of goods. This policy exists to keep the community safe and to ensure compliance with applicable laws in New Zealand and Australia. Where laws differ between New Zealand and Australia, the higher standard of protection applies.

---

## 2. Prohibited Content

You must not post, upload, share, or transmit any content that:

### 2.1 Illegal or Hazardous Items

- **Illegal items**: Items that are illegal to possess, sell, or give away under New Zealand or Australian law
- **Hazardous items**: Items that pose a risk to health or safety, including items that do not meet applicable product safety standards under the NZ Fair Trading Act 1986 or the Australian Consumer Law (ACL Schedule 2)
- **Recalled items**: Items subject to mandatory recall by any government authority in New Zealand or Australia
- **Restricted transfer items**: Items that cannot legally be transferred, including but not limited to:
  - Firearms and ammunition (NZ Arms Act 1983; AU state firearms legislation)
  - Prescription medications and controlled substances
  - Regulated plant material
  - Alcohol (where transfer restrictions apply)
  - CITES-regulated products (Convention on International Trade in Endangered Species)
  - Items subject to export controls

### 2.2 Intellectual Property Infringement

- **Counterfeit goods**: Items that are counterfeit, fake, or bear unauthorised trademarks
- **Pirated material**: Pirated software, media, or any other copyrighted material
- **Infringing content**: Photos, text, or other content that infringes another party's copyright, trademark, or other intellectual property rights

### 2.3 Harmful or Abusive Content

- **Defamatory content**: False statements of fact that damage another person's reputation
- **Harassment and threats**: Content intended to intimidate, harass, bully, or threaten any person
- **Hate speech**: Content that promotes hatred, discrimination, or violence against any person or group based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, or age
- **Non-consensual intimate images**: Intimate images of any person shared without their consent (a criminal offence under the Harmful Digital Communications Act 2015 (NZ), s.22A)
- **Doxxing**: Publishing another person's personal information (address, phone number, workplace, or other identifying details) without their consent
- **Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)**: Any content depicting the sexual exploitation or abuse of minors. We will report such content to the relevant authorities immediately.

### 2.4 Fraudulent or Deceptive Content

- **Scam listings**: Listings created with the intent to defraud or deceive other users
- **Misleading descriptions**: Deliberately false or misleading item descriptions, photos, or condition claims
- **Spam**: Unsolicited or repetitive content, bulk postings, or promotional material
- **Advertisements**: Commercial advertising, marketing, or solicitation (this is a free sharing platform, not a marketplace)

### 2.5 Malicious Content

- **Malware**: Viruses, trojans, spyware, or any other malicious software or code
- **Phishing**: Content designed to trick users into revealing personal information or credentials

## 3. Prohibited Conduct

You must not:

- **Sell items or request payment**: This is a free sharing platform. No monetary transactions, barter arrangements, or "donations" in exchange for items
- **Create fake or misleading listings**: Including listings for items you do not possess or intend to give away
- **Create multiple accounts**: To circumvent bans, suspensions, restrictions, or rate limits
- **Access other accounts**: Attempt to access other users' accounts or private data
- **Reverse-engineer or interfere**: Reverse-engineer, decompile, disassemble, or otherwise interfere with the App's infrastructure, code, or security measures
- **Circumvent security**: Bypass, disable, or circumvent rate limits, security measures, moderation systems, or access controls
- **Impersonate others**: Impersonate other users, or falsely represent yourself as GrabFree staff, administrators, or moderators
- **Use automated tools**: Use bots, scripts, scrapers, or other automated means to access the App, collect data, or interact with the App's systems
- **Engage in unlawful activity**: Use the App for any purpose that violates applicable New Zealand or Australian law
- **Abuse moderation systems**: Submit false reports, frivolous flags, or bad-faith community votes to target other users

## 4. Enforcement

We enforce this AUP through a graduated response system. The specific action taken depends on the severity and frequency of the violation.

### 4.1 Enforcement Actions

| Severity | Action | Example |
|----------|--------|---------|
| Minor / first offence | Content removal + warning | Accidental spam, mildly misleading listing |
| Moderate / repeated | Content removal + temporary suspension | Repeated policy violations after warning |
| Serious | Content removal + permanent ban | Harassment, fraud, illegal items |
| Criminal | Content removal + permanent ban + report to authorities | CSAM, non-consensual intimate images, credible threats |

### 4.2 Content Removal

We may remove any content at our sole discretion, with or without notice, and without any liability to you. Content removal does not require a violation to have occurred — we may remove content that we believe, in our reasonable judgment, is inappropriate for the platform.

### 4.3 Account Actions

- **Warning**: A formal notice that a specific action violated this AUP. Warnings are recorded on your account.
- **Temporary suspension**: Your account is suspended for a specified period. During suspension, you cannot access the App.
- **Permanent ban**: Your account is permanently terminated and you are prohibited from creating a new account.

### 4.4 Notification

When we take enforcement action, we will notify you via email with the reason for the action and, where applicable, the duration of any suspension.

### 4.5 Relationship to IP Takedown Process

Enforcement under this AUP is tracked separately from the content takedown process described in our [Terms of Use](TERMS_OF_USE.md), Section 17. However, posting content that infringes another party's intellectual property rights may result in both an AUP enforcement action and a strike under the repeat infringer policy.

## 5. Reporting Violations

### 5.1 How to Report

You may report content or conduct that violates this AUP through:

- **In-app reporting**: Use the report/flag function available on listings, board posts, and user profiles
- **Email**: Contact us at **dev@aiconsult.co.nz** with details of the violation

### 5.2 Report Categories

When reporting, please select the most appropriate category:

- Inappropriate or offensive content
- Spam or advertising
- Prohibited or illegal items
- Scam or fraud
- Harassment or threats
- Intellectual property infringement
- Duplicate listings
- Wrong categorisation
- Other violation

### 5.3 What Happens After a Report

Reports are reviewed by our moderation team. We will take appropriate action based on the nature and severity of the violation. We do not disclose the identity of reporters to the reported user. We are not obligated to take action on every report, but we review all reports received.
